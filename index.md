# Welcome to the Badger Biodata Initiative Official Website!

## The Badger Biodata Initiative Mission Statement

Badger Biodata Initiative is directed to those interested in programming projects related to biology and biological data. The goal is to create a space to organize groups of members to collaborate, create notable projects, learn together, and for each member to gain experience. Our promise to each member is to allow for the opportunity to learn and gain experience with programming languages, git, github, and useful libraries.

## What we do

 - Build Biological Data Projects
 - Learn & Use Industry-Relevant Tools
 - Explore Bio-computational and Bioinformatical Concepts
 - Establish Connections and Collaborate
